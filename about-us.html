About Us

Manufacturing Wellness. Delivering Trust.

Science-Driven Nutraceutical Manufacturing Partner

Incorporated on 19th April 2024, Bask Ray India Pvt. Ltd. is a visionary nutraceutical company committed to developing and manufacturing premium-quality nutraceutical products for domestic and international markets. Inspired by the Sanskrit word "Bhaskar", meaning the Sun, Bask Ray symbolizes energy, purity, vitality, and the power of science to improve lives through innovative wellness solutions.

At Bask Ray, we believe wellness is not merely a destination—it is a lifelong journey guided by science, inspired by nature, and strengthened by uncompromising quality.

We are a GMP-oriented nutraceutical manufacturing company dedicated to producing innovative, safe, and effective health supplements. Our expertise includes formulation development, product registration support, analytical testing, packaging, and commercial manufacturing.

By combining scientific expertise, advanced manufacturing technology, and stringent quality systems, we help brands successfully launch nutraceutical products across global markets.

Driven by innovation and excellence, Bask Ray offers comprehensive nutraceutical solutions including custom formulation development, contract manufacturing, private labeling, regulatory support, and end-to-end product development for brands worldwide.
