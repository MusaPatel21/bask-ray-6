About Us

Empowering Wellness Through Nature and Innovation

Incorporated on April 19, 2024, Bask Ray is a visionary nutraceutical company committed to delivering premium-quality products across domestic and international markets. Inspired by “Bhaskar” — the Sun, we aim to bring energy, vitality, and rejuvenation into everyday life through safe and effective nutraceutical solutions.

Our mission is to develop and manufacture scientifically formulated nutraceutical products that enhance health and well-being while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and innovation.
